The actual fine-tuned Qwen3.5-2B fall-detection model, running entirely on your GPU via WebGPU (llama.cpp/wllama). No server — your video never leaves your device.

⚠️ Your browser doesn't expose WebGPU. Use Chrome/Edge 121+ or Firefox Nightly to run the model. You can still browse the examples below (verdicts load once the model is running).
~1.9 GB download on first use, then cached. Runs locally.
